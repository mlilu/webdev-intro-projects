Zoro

This is Zoro, named after the One Piece swordsman, he is a little over a year old now. He was found in an alleyway and given to me by a friend who couldn't care for him at the time. I was so excited to add him to my family, he is officially my first feline pet. He's a shy guy who takes time to get used to new things, but super sweet and cuddly once he's comfortable. Zoro is smart and cautious, but gets into mischeif with his friend Faye quite often. He loves his bird toy, dinner time, and is a master at loafing. He's definitley a sucker for belly rubs, and he always gets them because how can anyone deny that cute furry boy.

Faye

Faye, named after another anime character, Faye Valentine from Cowboy Bepop, is an energetic, social girl. She has big eyes, and white mittens on at all times. She is very cuddly, loves attention, and will let you know it. She is, more often than not, a trouble maker in the house. It is so hard to stay mad at that cute face for long though. Like Zoro, Faye was given to me by a friend after being found in another alley. She was very cautious at first, but since she opened up, she has been the sweetest and most playful cat I've ever met. She loves butt pats and knocking drinks over any chance she gets.
